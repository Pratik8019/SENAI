# SentinelAI Pricing Policy

## Overview
SentinelAI offers three pricing tiers designed for teams of all sizes.

## Plans

### Starter — $49/month per seat
- Up to 5 team members
- 1,000 emails/month processing
- Basic AI classification
- Email-only support
- 7-day data retention for analytics

### Professional — $149/month per seat
- Up to 25 team members
- 10,000 emails/month processing
- Advanced AI with ReAct agent
- RAG-powered responses
- Priority support (4hr SLA)
- 90-day analytics retention
- Custom knowledge base

### Enterprise — Custom pricing
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited email processing
- Full agentic AI suite
- Dedicated success manager
- 24/7 phone support (1hr SLA)
- Custom integrations
- On-premise deployment option
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Unlimited data retention

## Add-ons
- **Web Intelligence Module**: +$29/seat/month
- **Custom AI Model Training**: +$499/month flat
- **API Access (external)**: +$99/month, includes 50k API calls

## Billing
- All plans billed monthly or annually (20% discount for annual)
- 14-day free trial on Starter and Professional
- Enterprise requires annual commitment
- Overages billed at $0.01/email beyond plan limits

## Refund-Eligible Scenarios
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of initial purchase. See refund_policy.md for details.
