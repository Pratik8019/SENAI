# SentinelAI Refund Policy

## Eligibility
Refunds are available under the following conditions:

### Full Refund (100%)
- Request within 14 days of initial purchase (trial period)
- Service was completely unavailable for >48 consecutive hours
- Billing error by SentinelAI

### Partial Refund (Pro-rated)
- Request within 30 days of purchase
- Documented service issues affecting core functionality
- Unresolved P1/P2 support tickets beyond SLA commitments

### No Refund
- Requests after 30 days
- Customer-caused issues or misconfigurations
- Change of business needs / no longer needed
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Add-on services after activation

## Process
1. Submit refund request via support@sentinelai.com or through the dashboard
2. Include: Account ID, reason, affected dates, relevant ticket numbers
3. Review completed within 5 business days
4. Approved refunds processed within 10 business days
5. Refunds issued to original payment method

## Enterprise Customers
Enterprise customers with custom contracts should refer to their specific agreement terms. Contact your dedicated account manager for refund inquiries.

## Chargebacks
If you file a chargeback without first contacting our support team, we reserve the right to suspend your account and contest the chargeback. Please reach out to us first — we want to resolve issues directly.

## Annual Plan Cancellations
Annual plans cancelled mid-term are eligible for a pro-rated refund minus a 15% early termination fee, unless cancelled within the first 30 days.
